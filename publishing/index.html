How the Publishing came to be

The Inside Story Series

#MamaNyoSquad Publishing started off as a collection of stories from various Venuses who have their own story to tell as they make their stay at the Venus Island.

Stories full of drama, mystery, and hope -- all resides here.

Since its first Inside story, its writing technique has evolved over time -- making it the root of what the Main Story is today.


Pictured: Admirant of the Present - Tsukushi's Inside Story

The Brand

#MamaNyoSquad Publishing is a collective mind -- resembling the experiences of the Venuses and alike.

At its core, is the representation that will always be bound through space and time. The chemistry between its prominent characters -- Main and Sub (Nyotengu and Helena) -- became the footprint of how this brand came to be: All sides, all universal in their own right.

Correlating to Venus Island Diaries, they play the biggest role in redefining the history of the island by representing themselves.

Home of Custom Lore and Beyond

From the creative mind of its Author -- thebelovedmoon -- #MamaNyoSquad Publishing aims to reinvent the DEAD OR ALIVE Series in order to be inclusive and applicable to many.

Housing the universality that redefines how we perceive Xtreme Venus Vacation -- Venus Island Diaries, and the heart of the Tengu that symbolizes a new hope -- the upcoming story The Tengu in Me; #MamaNyoSquad Publishing's goal is to touch the hearts of the readers through the lore that matters to everyone.

Universality, Redefined

#MamaNyoSquad Publishing aims to be universal where readers can read it at their own pace. For this, the publications in which it helmed are available on the following formats: Wattpad (for official publications), Revue, Google Docs, XPS (Open XML Paper Specifications) and PDF (Portable Document Format).

We're working on other availabilities, such as a separate page for all the publications in case others don't work out for you!

Expanding the Ventures

Like the formidable duo of DOA -- Main and Sub (Nyotengu and Helena) -- #MamaNyoSquad Publishing is constantly expanding its universe for a common bond... a bond that's notable throughout history.

Meet the Author

thebelovedmoon

thebelovedmoon is the General Manager of #MamaNyoSquad, and the main author behind #MamaNyoSquad Publishing. His current focus in terms of this caliber is Venus Island Diaries, as he spent his time to reinvent the story through its experiences as a whole.

While not on his duty as G.Mgr, he's the man of many sorts -- Graphic Designer (PowerPoint), Composer, Gamer, PR Manager, and Resident VTuber Supporter!

You may know him for instances such as ReactSquad (home of ReactOS-PH and ReactOS Italia), Princess Connect! Re:Dive (as Mana Senri), or VTubers such as the most-notable Hina Oujo as one of the Community Moderators.

His interest in writing first stemmed from his experience in DEAD OR ALIVE Xtreme Venus Vacation (DOAXVV) and his analysis on its Episodes. When all of his ideas came to light, he made the decision to put everything to execution -- making its initial mark as Venus Island Diaries through its first Inside Story named "Deep Arrogance - Nagisa's Inside Story". That and many others that came after that became the inspiration for the first rendition of its Main Story. He then since rewrote the story last April 2022 since his writing style has evolved a lot with every proceeding Inside Stories. Since then, he devoted his whole writing career to finishing the Main Story in a few years since its rewrite.

He's also working on two other stories not aligned with the Publishing: Life after Mahotan ～ まほたん後の人生 ～ (under tbm publishing co.) and 346: Scourge (under Sumiyoi Publisher's Pact).
