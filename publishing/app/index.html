Official App - Release Notes
v0.2.8-prerelease


Splash screen

Improved from v0.2.5-prerelease

We've implemented the Splash Screen to introduce the users to the app! We've also improved the alignment of the layouts especially on the logo to make it look like what Google Apps look like before Android 12's splash screen implementation.

We're working on bringing that implementation in later pre-releases.

App translations

Introduced in v0.2.5-prerelease

We've introduced an initial batch of language translations aiming to boost our reach to international readers! At the moment, we can only translate our original programs to Japanese.

Interested to see the translation magic? Change your language in your device settings!


Main Story button

Improved from v0.2.5-prerelease

We've improved the button placement for a quick redirect to our Main Story from its first public pre-release! In reflection, our site also inherited the same FAB (Floating Action Button) design!

In addition, our logo replaced the title text!


A OneUI-like look and feel

Improved from v0.2.5-prerelease

Natively, we're implementing the card shadow to coherent with the Material Design basis. But since we're transitioning to a OneUI-like look and feel, we've turned the shadow off!

For developers: card_view:cardElevation for Android is the same as box-shadow for the web, all it needs is some tinkering to suit more of your theming needs.
