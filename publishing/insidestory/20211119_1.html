Published 19 November 2021

As Nyotengu’s birthday is just around the corner, the stage is set for the new Owner to help out the three Admirers for the sake of the Venus Island.


POV: Nyotengu

As I glance over the sunset, I begin to wonder what I’ll be doing for my birthday. Tomorrow’s the start of my special week, so I assume the girls are ready to get prepared.

Slumping myself on the ground, I almost went for a swim as Fiona approached me from the right. “Nyo? Can I have you for a minute?”

Turning to gaze at her direction, I nod. “Sure.”

At the café, Monica, Misaki and Kanna are waiting at their seats. “Tomorrow’s your special week, how would you want it to be?” Misaki asked.

As Fiona and I take our seat, I begin to think deeply about it. “Since everyone considers me as the sexiest of them all, I guess we could have the theme to be just sexiness. You know how that works in my favor.”

“That’s not the point,” Monica contradicts, trying to aid in Misaki’s clarification. “What she’s trying to say is how we want your birthday to be in terms of its memorableness and novelty.”

“Fine,” I submit, going back to deeply thinking about it. “I guess if we have to take Ona-dono into consideration...”

“I’m sure he’ll remember every event as the memorable one,” Fiona said. “Look at how he’s trying his best to make me remember the best memories I had... up to the point with Tsukushi, however.”

“Speaking of Tsukushi,” I interrupt, letting my wings disappear in a white glow. “How is she?”

“Well, Ona has been taking care of her for the past few years, so I guess she felt better, at least.” Misaki hypothesizes as she hadn’t been instructed to inspect the Suite yet due to respecting their privacy.

“I guess we’ll just wait for them,” Kanna fidgets her hands. “That always works.”

“Perhaps,” Fiona reiterates. “I guess we should plan ahead of schedule to avoid any last-minute delays.”

“Absolutely,” I said, not knowing that his sweetie Helen is already approaching our direction. As soon as she does, she gently pats my head.

“Main?” Helena asks.

“S- sub?” I shriek, turning my back.

“What... how did that happen?” Monica stands in shock. I gotta admit, she rarely heard those before... those were some weird nicknames that our previous Owner used to call us. We got Miumiyu for Marie, Kaka for Kanna, heck! Even Monica got her own nickname: Monii.

Fiona has some interesting nicknames: Initially Fio, she has Hime-sama as it implies that she’s a Princess. Like, literally.

Misaki is no stranger to nicknames, especially when it comes to outfits she wears. I can still remember the Atelier Ryza × DOAXVV Collaboration where she wears Ryza’s outfit, therefore earning her nickname as Resident Ryza to just Reisalin--a nod to the main character Reisalin Stout.

...

Wait, why are we discussing nicknames? Oh that’s right, I’m the Main, Helen’s the Sub! We’re basically Main and Sub! I don’t know why that’s relevant...

“Pardon the interruption, but Elise and Sayuri want to see you,” Helen said in a straightforward manner. “I think that relates to your upcoming birthday party.”

“Sure, I’ll be with you in a hot minute.”

Turning to face the girls, I notify, “Alright, I should head out as Elise and Big Sis will be expecting my presence in a short minute. I’ll come back later so that we’ll discuss further details.”

“See you!” Misaki waved her hand goodbye, smiling as she looked at Fiona. Kanna beams at me as I leave the scene with Helen. Monica is still confused about what happened.

As we head straight inside the mansion, Lobelia approaches us from behind. I can only assume she is also invited for the meeting. Hitomi and Kokoro follow suit, only to change direction as they race up the stairs. As we head inside the Auditorium (seriously, this was among the other rooms planned to be added during the renovation period where Ona-dono was still recovering and Helen wasn’t with us), Elise and Sayuri can be seen in their casual outfits, complete with a tiara on their head.

“Mama Nyo,” Sayuri greeted with a smile on her face. “Tomorrow’s your special week~”

“I know,” I chuckle. “I need to be prepared for what’s about to happen next, though. What’s our agenda?”

“We already had the plans ahead of preparations. Now we’ll just need his approval to get started,” said Elise, handing the folder to my direction. Inside were a lot of write-ups and graphics that she made by hand. “Sayuri helped me with the event planning and where to put the decorations.”

“Isn’t that a bit too much? I mean, we usually didn’t cram the whole island with decorations,” I ask with a look of concern on my face as I scan through the folder containing some paperwork.

Helena pats my shoulder. “I mean, that would be possible; but, as Elise just said, we need his thoughts and approval first before we begin.”

“You’re right,” I said, closing the folder and handing it back to Elise. “At least we need to get the tables and chairs ready.”

“Good idea,” Sayuri nods. “I’ll get started with them right away.”
