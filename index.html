HM King Charles III has been crowned King of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland. This page has been updated to commemorate the historic coronation of TM King Charles III and Queen Consort Camilla as we begin another era of reign for all of UK.





An Open Message to the Royal Family

A wonderous day to the Royal Household and beyond! On behalf of #MamaNyoSquad, I congratulate TM King Charles III and Queen Consort Camilla for their recent coronation last 6 May 2023! It has been quite a journey that both of you have taken on despite the controversies that happen in the past, but we all know that with the perseverance that both of you have attained, the Royal Family will continue to live on for generations to come.

As we embark on this historic moment, let us embark on another journey that will become the new age of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, and we hope that the relations inherited from HM Queen Elizabeth II will always remain.

Godspeed! God Save the King, God Save the Queen!
