KT Disclosure

as of 26 January 2023 JST

View this document in: English | Japanese

KOEI TECMO GAMES Co., Ltd. has just informed the public regarding the recent police report filed by the Kanagawa Prefectural Police to the Yokohama District Public Prosecutor's Office on 24 January 2023 regarding the copyright infringement against a 44 year-old individual who uses the materials (characters, logo, etc.) to sell as body pillow covers.

According to the aforementioned report, 22 body pillow covers were confirmed to include copyrighted materials straight from DEAD OR ALIVE 6, including pictures of various characters, like Marie Rose. In turn, these merchandise are being repurposed for sale online.

As of the report, the suspect has been sent to prosecution, and will await pending charges against the suspect. KT remains committed on dealing with copyright infringements like this whilst serving the best-quality games to the audience. The PDF (in Japanese) related to this report can be seen below.

To see how #MamaNyoSquad deals with KT-related materials, see "KT Disclosure" at the Policies page.
