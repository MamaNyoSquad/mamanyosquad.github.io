#MamaNyoSquad observes Holy Week

published 28 March 2024 UTC

As we observe Holy Week around the world, we at #MamaNyoSquad will be unwinding, which means our activities may be slow and some contents are out of date.

In this article

  • In observance of Holy Week
  • PR updates on X
  • Website changes
  • Maintenance Tracker
  • Important notes

In observance of Holy Week

#MamaNyoSquad joins the world in observing Holy Week. Due to this, we the SquadMates are taking our time off to unwind and have a well-deserved rest.

However, we remain committed to our vision of keeping the Dead or Alive spirit alive through DOAXVV and beyond despite these events. For this, we have laid out a plan on how we'll be utilizing our activities during this event.

PR updates on X

As a result of this observance, most updates from us and our associated accounts (like @VenusVIDiaries) will be on minimum until Easter Monday. However, updates regarding DOAXVV and Satou Akemi will still be available via reposts.

Some important updates will still be sent as mandated, however, they may take time to be delivered to the audience.

Website changes

Website updates may be behind upstream in observance of Holy Week. This includes updates to our website layouts, events, and DOAXVV showcase on the home page.

In addition, Venus Island Diaries will also be at minimal activity during this event. Some updates -- like web design updates and new Inside Stories -- will be expected to release after the event.

Maintenance Tracker

Updates to the Maintenance Tracker will be slow during the event. In some instances, contents related to it may be already out of date.

When that happens, consult either Japanese Edition or Global Edition for any updates regarding upcoming/ongoing Maintenance Routines. We'll be updating the Tracker with the next Maintenance Routine should we skip one.

Important notes

Starting from Holy Week 2024, this Blog will be referenced whenever such an annual event will occur.

This Blog may be updated to include additional updates regarding our other frameworks. Don't worry -- we'll be disclosing them over on X should we add them.

And lastly, ensure proper etiquette when observing this event. Also, use this event to take some time off and unwind -- you'll be needing them as soon as work week starts next week.

We hope you enjoy the rest of Holy Week!
