An Open Message to the Royal Family

A wonderous day to the Royal Household and beyond! On behalf of #MamaNyoSquad, I congratulate TM King Charles III and Queen Consort Camilla for their recent coronation last 6 May 2023! As we embark on this historic moment, let us embark on another journey that will become the new age of the United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, and we hope that the relations inherited from HM Queen Elizabeth II will always remain.

Godspeed! God Save the King, God Save the Queen!
