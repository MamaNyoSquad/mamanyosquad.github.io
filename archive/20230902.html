SINoALICE Global will end soon.

With just moments away from sealing the Library, SINoALICE Global thanks its players for their unending desire to support the game through various means. From its developers -- SQUARE ENIX and Pokelabo -- they thank the fans and everyone for making their dreams possible, and that they will usher forward with their future endeavors.


Letter from the Producer

Attached is the exact copy of the letter as it appeared in the in-game notice. Timestamps have been modified to conform with the UTC timezone.

Thank you for playing SINoALICE.

This is Pokelabo Inc. SINoALICE Producer Shogo Maeda.

We understand that this announcement might come as a surprise to our users, but as previously mentioned in the End of Service Notice, it is my deepest regret to inform you that we will be concluding our Service on 2023.11.15 4:59 (UTC).
We have devoted ourselves to devising and implementing ideas that would allow players to enjoy the game for years to come, but despite our best efforts, we have fallen short of sustaining this goal. We deeply apologize for the situation.

The global version began operation back in July 2020, and thanks to all our users we've managed to keep it going for over three years.
We were able to hold the World Gran Colosseum twice through the support of our Global players, and personally, I was truly moved by the way people from around the world, including players from the JP version, connected through the event.

The main story will conclude with the currently released Act of Elimination.
Furthermore, as part of our operations through to October 31st, we have prepared the following events for our Last Big Thanks Campaign.
-Big Thanks Free Grimoire x11
-Big Thanks Mission
-The Last Colosseum Sin
Plus others.
We hope you continue to enjoy the game leading up to The Last Colosseum.

Lastly,
while were there moments when we might have inconvenienced our users, we're grateful to have been able to operate for more than three years.
We want to extend a sincere thank you to all the users who supported and livened up our activities on Discord, the Royal Creators who contributed to the excitement by streaming SINoALICE on platforms like YouTube, and to all the users who consistently enjoyed playing the game. We can't thank you all enough for being a part of the community.
Although we're nearing the end of our service, we hope you make the most of SINoALICE Global through to the very end. Thank you.

Pokelabo Inc. Producer
Shogo Maeda
