In 1989...

★ ★ ★ ★

4 June 1989 saw one of the most gruesome moments in human democracy where authoritarianism runs apparent. Following series of demonstrations from the student movement, the People's Liberation Army -- backed by the Chinese Communist Party in retaliation to the cries for democracy -- has opened fire on the demonstrators “in an attempt to regain control of the area.”

In reality, such open-fires have demonstrated that the CCP will NEVER listen to the pleas of the demonstrators in the name of democracy. This has been in practice even today -- with tight suppressions from the government against any form of peace and democracy.

Remember the South China Sea?

The Tank Man

★ ★ ★ ★

On 5 June 1989, a historic moment that sparked the cries for democracy once more has occurred: An unknown individual attempts to obstruct a line of tanks that closes in on the Tiananmen Square, which had “slowed down the crackdown,” but he has been arrested for “political hooliganism.”

To this day, he has still not been identified; and all of his efforts in the name of democracy have been suppressed by the CCP’s “Great Firewall.”

We need answers.

Despite all the suppressions, many people still fight for the truth and justice of their loved ones one way or another. As long as the fire of democracy keeps on burning, the cries of justice will always carry on.

#MamaNyoSquad, The Ortega Project, and tbmassoc stand in solidarity of the relatives of those who lost their lives during the massacre, and strongly urge the Chinese Communist Party to give us EXACTLY the justice the victims deserved.
