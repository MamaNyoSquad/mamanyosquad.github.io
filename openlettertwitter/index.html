AN OPEN LETTER TO TWITTER

Issued 20 November 2022 UTC

Twitter has became the home of many opportunities that we don't know is possible. From memes to information about certain brands, and beyond -- it became the hub of what's known as the "Town Square of the People".

Of course, we won't even forget its mishaps and things that reshaped the platform throughout the years on a negative note -- drama, controversies, and even as far as exploitations that harm the users.
